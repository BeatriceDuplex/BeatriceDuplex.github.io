House rules

The only thing we ask is to be respectful of the apartment and the neighbours. The furniture in this apartments has an history starting from 1995. Many people enjoyed it, and we hope that you and other guest will enjoy it after your stay.

Accidents can always happen, if something breaks or you find it broken, just inform us.

Please do not smoke in the apartment, but feel free to use the balconies.

Television

Dining room

Switch on the television. Press manually (no remote control) the on button of the television. This button is located on the front of the television on the bottom right corner.

Switch on the decoder. Use the black remote control “TechniSat” to switch on the decoder. Use the same remote to change channels and volume.

Living room

Switch on the television. Use the grey remote control “Panasonic” and press the power button with a long press. Alternatively press manually the on button of the television. This button is located on the top of the television.

Select the HTMI source. Press the TV/AV button on the grey remote control “Panasonic” and select HTMI 1. This button is located on the top right of the grey Panasonic remote control. Alternatively, press manually the TV/AV button on the top right of the television.

Switch on the decoder. Use the black remote control “TechniSat” to switch on the decoder. Use the same remote to change channels and volume.

House key

You will receive the access code to the key-box upon your reservation. The key-box is located next to the entry door of the apartment (third floor, right door). The key-box contains an single key used to open all doors of the building: building, apartment and sky room.

Remember to deposit the key in the key-box when checking-out. A not returned key requires a change in the locks. The costs are charged on the guest.

Wine fridge

Feel free to cool your drinks in the wine fridge in the living room. Connect the withe electricity plug situated on the right side of the book shelf.

Suggested temperatures:

  • 18 degrees for red wines
  • 12 degrees for withe wines
  • 10 degrees for beers
  • 7 degrees for champagne

Trash

The following materials can be recycled:
  • Glass
  • Aluminium
  • PET

All other trash should be placed in the official orange trash bag. The orange trash bags can be bought (individually) in the main shops, for example Coop or Volg.

Trash can be deposited in the recycling station in front of Coop.

Please, remember to bring out the trash when you leave the apartment.
